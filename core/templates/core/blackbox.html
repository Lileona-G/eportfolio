Evidence

The Mystery Black Box is a big box that was presented to me and my class by our teacher. Me and my class’s initial observations about The Mystery Black Box was that when water was poured into the tube sticking out that less would come out.But then if you were to pour the same amount again this time less would come out. Me and my team's approach to finding out what’s going on inside this box is sharing all of our theories based on the evidence that we collected so far. We were mostly trying to demonstrate that we thought inside of the black box that there was some kind of sea-saw method going on although we weren't able to demonstrate it. We tested our model by measuring water and pouring it into the hole at the top of our box, then measuring how much came out.We did this multiple times and wrote down the data. When collecting our data we realized that if you would pour a certain amount of water inside a little less would usually come out.For example when we poured 800 ml in 790 ml came out.While when we poured 700 ml in 520 ml came out.The last text our box stated to have a lot of leakage.For example 1000 ml was poured and only 480 ml came out.Our last observation was that our project needs to be held in order to function to correctly.
